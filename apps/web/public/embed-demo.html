Klickenya Embed Loader — Demo

This page simulates an external business website (Squarespace/Wix/plain HTML). It loads /embed.js once and declares widgets with data-klickenya-tool attributes. The loader injects the iframe and auto-resizes it via the klickenya:resize postMessage bridge. Change data-slug to a real published menu/property slug on your environment.

Reservation widget — data-klickenya-tool="reservation"

Menu widget (dark) — data-klickenya-tool="menu" data-theme="dark"

Menu + booking combo — data-klickenya-tool="restaurant"

Property booking — data-klickenya-tool="booking" (route ships in Phase 3)
