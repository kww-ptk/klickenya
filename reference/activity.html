About this experience

Wake before dawn and head deep into the Maasai Mara National Reserve with James — one of Kenya's most celebrated safari guides. The full-day game drive begins at sunrise, when the plains are at their most magical and the big cats are most active.

You'll traverse the open savanna in a custom Land Cruiser, tracking lion prides, watching elephant herds cross the Mara River, and witnessing the extraordinary biodiversity of one of Africa's most iconic ecosystems. Around mid-morning, James will guide you to a secluded spot for a full cooked bush breakfast — coffee, eggs, fresh fruit, and the sound of the wild.

The afternoon brings cheetah tracking and a visit to a Maasai village, before returning at sunset with memories that will last a lifetime.

Your day

5:30 AM

Hotel pickup from Nairobi

Meet your driver at your hotel lobby. 3.5 hour drive through the Rift Valley to the Mara.

9:00 AM

Enter the Maasai Mara

Gate check-in, briefing from James. Begin the morning game drive through the northern Mara.

10:30 AM

Bush breakfast

Freshly cooked breakfast in the bush — eggs, sausages, fresh fruit, coffee and juice.

12:00 PM

Midday game drive — Big Five

Track lions, leopards, and herds. Visit the Mara River for potential hippo and crocodile sightings.

2:00 PM

Maasai Village visit

Optional cultural visit to a local Maasai manyatta. Meet the community and buy handcrafts.

4:30 PM

Sunset game drive

Golden hour tracking — the best light for photography. Cheetah and hyena are most active.

6:00 PM

Return to Nairobi

Drop back at your hotel by approximately 9:30 PM, full of incredible memories.
