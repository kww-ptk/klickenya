About this space

Welcome to the Sunset Loft — a beautifully curated studio apartment on the 18th floor of the iconic 14 Riverside building in Westlands, Nairobi. Floor-to-ceiling windows frame a breathtaking panoramic view of the Nairobi skyline, bathed in warm amber light as the sun sets over the city.

The space features premium Kenyan hardwood floors, a fully equipped kitchen with Italian espresso machine, high-speed WiFi, and a private balcony perfect for morning coffee. The bedroom is separated by a sliding wooden partition, offering privacy while maintaining the open, airy feel of the loft.

Located 2 minutes walk from Westgate Mall and 5 minutes from the vibrant Westlands dining scene — over 40 restaurants within walking distance.
