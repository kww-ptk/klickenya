About this event

Kenya's most beloved outdoor music and lifestyle festival returns to Ngong Racecourse for its landmark 2026 edition. Blankets & Wine is the definitive afternoon-to-evening experience — spread your blanket on the grass, uncork a bottle, and let the music wash over you as the Nairobi skyline glows behind the stage.

This edition features an extraordinary lineup of Afrobeats, Afro-soul, R&B, and jazz artists from across East Africa and the continent. Curated food stalls from Nairobi's top restaurants, craft wine and spirits bars, artisan markets, and live art installations complete the experience.

Bring your blanket, your crew, and your appetite — for music, food, and pure Nairobi magic.

Frequently asked questions

What should I bring?
Bring your blanket or picnic mat, a valid ID for age-restricted areas, comfortable shoes, sunscreen for the afternoon, and a light jacket for the evening. Outside food and alcohol are not permitted.
Is there parking available?
Yes — Ngong Racecourse has 2,000+ parking spaces. Parking is free for all ticket holders. Shuttles from Westlands (every 30 mins) and the CBD (hourly) are also available from 1:30 PM.
Can I pay with M-Pesa?
Absolutely. Klickenya accepts M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfer. You'll receive a QR code ticket instantly via SMS and email after payment.
What's the refund policy?
Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before the event. 50% refund between 3–7 days. No refund within 72 hours. Tickets are fully transferable up to 24 hours before the event.
Is this event kid-friendly?
Children under 12 enter free with a paying adult. There is a dedicated family zone with activities. The alcohol areas are age-restricted (18+). The event is family-friendly until 8 PM.
